Frequently asked questions

Direct answers to the questions serious contractors usually ask before sending plans, models, or project workflow needs.

Who do you work with?
We work with general contractors and specialty trades on U.S. construction projects that need support with estimating, BIM coordination, document control, RFIs, change orders, TAKT planning, or project controls.
Can support be scoped to one service only?
Yes. Support can be scoped around a single need such as quantity takeoffs, coordination drawings, document control, RFI logs, change order backup, planning support, or a broader package across multiple workflows.
Do you support both preconstruction and active projects?
Yes. Some engagements are strictly preconstruction, such as estimating and takeoff support. Others are active project support, such as BIM coordination, RFIs, change documentation, TAKT workflow support, and controls tracking.
What kind of deliverables do clients typically receive?
Depending on the scope, deliverables may include takeoffs, drawing markups, BIM support files, coordination drawings, issue logs, document registers, RFI tracking, change order backup, planning structures, reporting templates, and workflow documentation tied to the live need.

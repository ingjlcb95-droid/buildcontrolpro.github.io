Frequently asked questions

Direct answers to the questions serious contractors usually ask before sending project details or workflow needs.

What software can you work with?
Depending on the workflow, support can align with Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud, Bluebeam, Revit, Navisworks, Excel, Smartsheet, Primavera P6, MS Project, and client-specific file structures.
Can support be scoped to one workflow only?
Yes. Support can be scoped around one need such as takeoffs, document control, RFI drafting and tracking, change documentation, BIM coordination support, TAKT planning, or project controls reporting.
What do deliverables usually look like?
Deliverables depend on scope, but may include quantity takeoffs, drawing markups, scope breakdowns, document logs, revision trackers, RFI logs, change backup packages, planning structures, and reporting templates.
Can you work with our internal team and file standards?
Yes. Support is meant to plug into the existing team, file naming rules, reporting cadence, and project priorities so work can move without unnecessary friction.
What kinds of project pressure are you best suited for?
We are most useful when estimating workload spikes, documentation starts slipping, change backup needs to become more defensible, coordination follow-through weakens, or reporting discipline starts falling behind the pace of the job.
What kinds of project types are you built for?
The positioning fits public works, private commercial, multifamily, roadway and infrastructure, municipal, healthcare, education, and other contractor-driven environments where documentation quality, scope control, and stronger records are critical to execution.
